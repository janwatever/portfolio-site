Trouble Through Town

Play as a shepherd and herd your sheep through the land to safety on the other side of town

Team Size : 5
Project Length : 3 months
Engine : Unity

Features
  • Herding sheep mechanic
  • Unique stressors in each area that causes sheep to panic and run away
  • Chase after runaway sheep and calm them back to the herd
  • 4 uniquely designed areas
Constraints
  • 1 week to brainstorm a game and pitch it
  • 11 weeks to fully design, develop and publish the game
  • 2 mandatory playtest builds during the 3rd and 7th week of development
  • It was the team's first attempt at creating a 3D game
Tasks
  • Designed game premise
  • Established art direction
  • Level design (Dark Forest level)
  • Cinematic cutscenes
  • Post-processing, Lighting and Particle effects
