Integrooving

23 October 2023

Developmental log of Integrooving

The game idea was developed for an assignment for a class in university.

Narrative

You're a CIA agent who has infiltrated a jazzy new world. Your job is to blend in to the funky beats of their society and steal their declaration of independence.

Gameplay
Rough outline

1. A combo sequence plays at the start of the level.
2. Hit the keys according to the beat in the next bar.

Gameplay
Gameplay sequence

Prompts are written on screen and what you should do to blend in. If you miss a beat, the crowd will stare you down and if there is too many miss beats, results in a game over.
